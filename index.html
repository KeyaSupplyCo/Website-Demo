About Keya Supply Co

We specialize in high-performance PET and PP strapping for North American industry. By sourcing directly from ISO-certified manufacturers and eliminating middlemen, we provide bulk wholesale solutions that combine factory-direct pricing with local reliability.

Heavy-Duty PET Straps

High-tensile polyester strapping with minimal stretch. Ideal for steel coils, lumber, construction materials, and industrial equipment. Weather-resistant for outdoor use.

AAR Approved Straps

Association of American Railroads certified strapping for rail freight. Smooth and embossed types — meeting all AAR specs for safe, compliant transit.

Polypropylene Straps

Lightweight, economical solution for light-to-medium loads. Excellent flexibility for carton bundling, newspaper packaging, and palletizing. Hand & machine grade available.

Custom Specifications

Eco-Friendly PET

Bulk Discounts

Industries We Serve

From automotive plants to lumber yards, our strapping solutions are engineered for the demands of heavy industry across the U.S. and Canada.

  • Automotive

    AAR-rated PET strapping for Tier 1 supplier pallet shipments across the Detroit corridor.

  • Metal Packaging

    High-tensile PET strapping engineered to secure heavy steel coils and sheet metal safely in transit.

  • Textile Industry

    PP strapping for baling and bundling textile goods — flexible, lightweight, and cost-effective at volume.

  • Tubing

    Reliable bundling and unitizing of tube and pipe products for safe storage and outbound shipping.

  • Bottling Industries

    Consistent, machine-compatible PP and PET strapping for beverage can and bottling plant palletization.

  • Building Material & Lumber

    Weather-resistant PET strapping built for outdoor lumber yards, construction supply, and long-haul loads.

  • Paver Industry

    Heavy-duty strapping solutions for securing paver blocks and hardscape materials in storage and distribution.

  • Glass & Ceramic

    Low-stretch PET strapping that protects fragile glass and ceramic loads without crushing or shifting.

  • Palletization

    Full-pallet and truckload strapping for logistics operators, 3PLs, and warehousing operations across North America.
